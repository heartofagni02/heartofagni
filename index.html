What We Do

Heart of Agni is dedicated to capturing and showcasing the vibrant life of our university through various media forms.

Media Production

Creating high-quality visual and audio content for faculty events, seminars, and activities.

News Coverage

Reporting on important events and achievements within the Faculty of Management & Commerce.

Event Management

Organizing and documenting academic and extracurricular events throughout the academic year.

Student Engagement

Providing opportunities for students to develop media skills and creative expression.
