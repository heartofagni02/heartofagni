Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Heart of Agni

How can I join the Heart of Agni media team?

We recruit new members at the beginning of each academic year. Announcements are made through faculty notice boards and our social media. You can also email us expressing your interest with a brief introduction and any relevant experience. No prior media experience is required - we provide training!

How do I request media coverage for my faculty event?

Please submit a request at least 2 weeks before your event by emailing us at heartofagni99@gmail.com. Include event details, date, time, venue, and specific coverage requirements. We prioritize faculty academic events, but also cover student activities and cultural events.

Can I use photos/videos taken by Heart of Agni for personal use?

Photos and videos we produce are primarily for faculty and university use. Students may request personal copies of images they appear in for non-commercial use. For any publication or commercial use, written permission is required. High-resolution images are available upon request.

Do you provide media equipment training for students?

Yes! We conduct regular workshops on photography basics, video editing, social media management, and content writing. These workshops are free for Faculty of Management & Commerce students. Check our social media or faculty notice boards for upcoming workshop schedules.

How can I collaborate with Heart of Agni for a project?

We welcome collaborations with other student societies, departments, and external organizations that align with our mission. Please send a detailed proposal to heartofagni99@gmail.com outlining the project scope, timeline, and mutual benefits.
